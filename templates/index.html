It was going to rain. The weather forecast didn't say that, but
the steel plate in his hip did. He learned over the years to
trust his hip over the weatherman. It was going to rain,
so he better get outside and prepare. This is the end.




You know that tingly feeling you get on the back of
your neck sometimes? I just got that feeling when talking
with her. You know I don't believe in sixth senses, but there is
something not right with her. I don't know how I know, I just do.
